Animal Classification using CNN

Animal Classification:


Animal classification using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) is a state-of-the-art approach that leverages the power of deep learning to categorize and identify various species based on their visual characteristics. CNNs are a class of artificial neural networks specifically designed to process and recognize patterns in images.The process begins with a large dataset of animal images, carefully labeled with their corresponding species. The CNN model then learns to extract essential features and patterns from the images through multiple layers of convolution, pooling, and activation functions. Convolutional layers use small filters to scan the input image, capturing different features like edges, textures, and shapes.
